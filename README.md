This project was done with QGIS, with the intent to practice using the QGIS model designer.

For this project, a food desert was defined as an area further than a 5-minute walk (500m) away from a supermarket or further than a 10-minute walk (1km) away from a hawker centre. This difference was made on the basis that it would be inconvenient to walk long distances while carrying groceries. To avoid including uninhabited areas, any area that has a population density less than the mean population density or was a park was excluded.

The datasets used were as follows:

Hawker centres: https://data.gov.sg/datasets/d_4a086da0a5553be1d89383cd90d07ecd/view

Supermarkets: https://data.gov.sg/datasets/d_cac2c32f01960a3ad7202a99c27268a0/view

Parks: https://data.gov.sg/datasets/d_0542d48f0991541706b58059381a6eca/view

Subzones: https://data.gov.sg/datasets/d_2d6793de474551149c438ba349a108fd/view and https://data.gov.sg/datasets/d_8594ae9ff96d0c708bc2af633048edfb/view
